## 15. Smart Follow-Ups That Don't Feel Spammy

**Leads go cold. Pixie remembers — and nudges gently, in the right voice, at the right time.**

### How it works
If a prospect goes silent after seeing a preview or getting a quote, Pixie follows up automatically:
- **2 hours later:** gentle check-in
- **12 hours later:** reminder + offer to split payment
- **23 hours later:** final note with a small discount (only if it makes sense)

Each message is written to match the customer's personality — casual for casual leads, professional for formal ones. No copy-pasted sales spam.

### What Pixie will *never* do
- Send 5 follow-ups in a row.
- Pretend today's the last day of an offer that isn't ending.
- Beg, guilt, or overpromise.
- Send follow-ups after you've explicitly told it to stop.

### For SEO leads
One single nudge 24 hours later, quoting the biggest issue found in their audit. Then silence. Because SEO buyers compare agencies, and a 5-message cascade reads as desperate.
