# Case Studies

## E-Commerce Transformation: Local Boutique Goes Online
**Industry:** Retail / Fashion
**Services:** E-Commerce Website, SEO, Digital Marketing

A local fashion boutique wanted to expand beyond their physical store. We built a complete e-commerce platform with:
- Modern, mobile-first design showcasing 500+ products
- Integrated payment processing (Stripe)
- Inventory sync with their POS system
- SEO optimization targeting local and national keywords

**Results:**
- 300% increase in monthly revenue within 6 months
- 15,000 monthly website visitors
- 45% of sales now come from online orders
- Top 3 Google ranking for key product categories

## Restaurant Chain: Digital Marketing Campaign
**Industry:** Food & Beverage
**Services:** Digital Marketing, Social Media, SEO

A regional restaurant chain with 5 locations wanted to increase foot traffic and online orders.

**What we delivered:**
- Social media strategy and content calendar
- Professional food photography and video content
- Google Ads campaign targeting local customers
- Instagram and TikTok marketing
- Google Business Profile optimization for all locations

**Results:**
- 150% increase in online orders
- 80,000 social media impressions per month
- 4.8 average Google rating across all locations
- 25% increase in foot traffic during campaign period
