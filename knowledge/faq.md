# Frequently Asked Questions

## What services does Bytes Platform offer?

Bytes Platform is a full-service digital agency specializing in:
- Custom website design and development
- Ecommerce store builds
- SEO campaigns (3-month engagements)
- Social media management (monthly packages)
- AI chatbot development

## How long does it take to build a website?

Timelines depend on the package:
- A 1–2 page starter site is typically delivered within 5–7 business days.
- A 3–5 page business website takes around 1–2 weeks.
- Ecommerce stores with custom features can take 2–4 weeks depending on the number of products and integrations.

We'll give you an estimated timeline before starting.

## What's included in a website build?

Every website we build includes:
- Mobile-responsive design
- Basic on-page SEO setup
- Speed optimization
- Contact form or CTA
- A revision period after delivery so you can request changes

Higher-tier packages include more pages, advanced SEO, Google Maps integration, and more.

## Do I need to provide my own content and images?

Not necessarily. We can write professional copy for your website based on your business details. If you have specific images or branding assets you'd like to use, send them over — otherwise we'll use high-quality stock imagery that fits your brand.

## How does the revision process work?

After we deliver your website or content, you get a revision period where you can request changes — things like text updates, color adjustments, layout tweaks, and more. The number of revision rounds depends on your package. We want you to be happy with the final product.

## What does an SEO campaign include?

Our SEO campaigns run for a minimum of 3 months and include:
- Technical site audit and fixes
- Keyword research and targeting
- On-page optimization (meta tags, headings, content)
- Off-page work like backlink building (Pro tier and above)
- Regular performance reports

Results typically start showing within 4–8 weeks depending on competition.

## What does social media management include?

Depending on your package, social media management includes:
- Custom content creation (posts, reels, stories)
- Content scheduling and publishing
- Hashtag and content strategy
- Community engagement and comment management
- Monthly analytics and performance reports

We manage Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and LinkedIn.

## Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. For packages over $200, we offer flexible payment plans:
- **$200–$500:** 2 payments (60% upfront, 40% on delivery)
- **$501–$1,500:** 2 or 3 payments
- **$1,501–$4,500:** 3 payments or monthly installments

The total price stays the same — no extra charges for splitting. First payment is required before we start.

No worries — that's what we're here for. Just tell us about your business and goals, and we'll recommend the right service and package. No pressure, no jargon.

## How do I get started?

Just message us here on WhatsApp! We'll ask a few questions about your business, recommend a package, and set up a quick 15-minute call with our project specialist to finalize everything.

## Do you work with international clients?

Yes. We work with clients worldwide. Pricing is in USD regardless of location. We communicate in English, Urdu, Hindi, Arabic, Spanish, French, and more.

## What happens after I approve the project?

Once you approve and make the first payment:
1. We begin working on your project immediately.
2. You'll receive progress updates at key milestones.
3. We deliver the first version for your review.
4. You request any changes during the revision period.
5. Final delivery and launch.

## Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes. You can start with a smaller package and upgrade anytime. It's often more cost-effective to bundle services upfront, but we're flexible.

## What if I need ongoing support after delivery?

We offer monthly maintenance plans that cover updates, fixes, and changes without extra invoices. Ask us about adding a retainer to your project.
